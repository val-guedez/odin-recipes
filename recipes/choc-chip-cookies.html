Follow the recipe below to eat cookies like the ones above :D

I have personally used this recipe to replicate the cookies you can get at subway (the yummy, chewy style), so if you like that style of cookie keep on reading!

Ingredients

  • 1 stick softened salt butter
  • 1/2 cup light brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/2 cups plain all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 1/2 cups semi sweet choc chips
